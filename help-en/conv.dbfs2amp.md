# conv.dbfs2amp


convert decibel full scale to amplitude

---

Note: db values &lt;= -144 are converted to 0
<br>


---


```


[ui.hsl @min -144 @max 6]
|
[F digits=8]
|
[dbfs->amp]
|
[F digits=8]
|
[amp->dbfs]
|
[F digits=8]

            
```
