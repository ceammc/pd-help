# data.mlist


multidimensional list container

---

Syntax: values are separated by spaces: (1 2 3) - list of 1, 2 and 3
Nested lists: (1 2 3 (nested liat values))
Symbols with spaces: (&#34;single item list&#34;)
<br>


---

            4)<br>

            empty, otherwise 0<br>
@size: number of
            elements in list<br>
