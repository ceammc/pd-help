time(ms): 
            echo time<br>

            coefficient<br>

---
properties:

@delay(ms): echo time<br>
@feedback: 
            feedback coefficient<br>
@drywet: proportion
            of mix between the original (dry) and &#39;effected&#39; (wet) signals. 0 - dry signal, 1 -
            wet<br>
@bypass: if set to 1 - bypass
            &#39;effected&#39; signal<br>
@active: on/off dsp
            processing<br>
