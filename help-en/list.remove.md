# list.remove

###### remove list element(s) at specified positions

*available since version:* 0.6

---




[![example](../examples/img/list.remove.jpg)](../examples/pd/list.remove.pd)

* **INDEXES**
list of indexes to remove. Negative indexes are also supported<br>
