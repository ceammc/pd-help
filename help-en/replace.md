# replace


Replace atoms in data stream

---

From/to values can be specified both as @from/@to properties or first and second
            arguments.
<br>


---


![example](examples/replace-example.jpg)

---
arguments:

FROM: replace subject<br>
TO: replace value<br>

---
properties:

@from: replace subject<br>
@to: replace value<br>
