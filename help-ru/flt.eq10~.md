# flt.eq10~

###### graphic 10 band equalizer

*доступно с версии:* 0.1

---


## информация
One band per octave. 5th-order Butterworth filters


[![example](../examples/img/flt.eq10~.jpg)](../examples/pd/flt.eq10~.pd)





## методы:

* **reset**
reset filter state to default<br>
